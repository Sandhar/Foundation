The Odyssey Leadership Foundation

Equipping Australia’s youth and educators to lead with integrity.

Namesake

We draw inspiration from “Odyssey” by Homer because his epic is a story about leadership when it matters most. They insist that authority is for the common good—as defined in ancient Greek custom of xenia, the duty to honour and protect the stranger; that resilience must be joined to prudent craft (métis) if people are to navigate storms; and that hubris—power without restraint—destroys households and cities alike. Odyssey exists to cultivate leaders who earn trust, steward power carefully, and account for the people whose lives their decisions impact.

“Whether you lead an army, a kingdom, a farm, or a smithy, take heed for those who toil beneath you. Someday you may be called upon to give an account of your treatment of them. Be prepared and be afraid.” – Homer

Vision

Provide every young Australian with the opportunity to speak with confidence and lead with integrity, strengthening their communities for the common good.

Mission

The Odyssey Leadership Foundation exists to equip Australia’s youth and educators with the skills and confidence to communicate clearly, lead ethically, and become the leaders of tomorrow.

Leadership Models and Tools

Our leadership models and tools equip emerging and existing leaders with concepts and practical strategies that help them lead effective teams and organisations in our 21st Century context.
